What is a Gold Loan for Business?

A gold loan for business is a type of loan that is secured against gold. The gold serves as collateral for the loan, and the loan amount is based on the value of the gold. Gold loans are typically used by small businesses to access capital for business purposes, with convenience.

Gold loans are typically repaid over a period of 12 months, and the interest rate onthe loan is fixed. The repayment schedule is flexible, and borrowers can make early repayments as well.
