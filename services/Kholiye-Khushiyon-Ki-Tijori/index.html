  • About Muthoot Finance

    The story of Muthoot Finance dates back to over seven decades, when a trading business was established by Shri. Ninan Mathai Muthoot in 1887. Almost 5 decades later, his son, Late Shri. M. George Muthoot, founded the Gold Loan business in 1939.

    We are India’s No. 1 Most Trusted Financial Services Brand for 6 years since 2016 by TRA’s Brand Trust Report. We are also India’s largest gold financing company in terms of loan portfolio. We are a reputed ‘Systematically Important Non-Deposit taking NBFC’ & have recently been certified as an “Upper Layer NBFC” as well by Reserve Bank of India. As part of our core business, we provide business loans secured by gold jewellery, or gold loans. We primarily cater to individuals who possess gold jewellery but cannot access formal credit within a reasonable time, or for whom credit may not be available at all. We help our customers meet unanticipated or other short-term liquidity requirements.
